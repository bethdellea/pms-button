About the Project

an app where you can automatically transfer your menstrual cramps to a politician who does not support reproductive health

— Lindsay Zoladz (@lindsayzoladz) June 27, 2016
Insipiration for the PMS Button.

It's pretty obvious why something like this would be important to people who menstruate. These issues should also be important to those who don't, too, but that's not what this app is about.

Press the button when your PMS symptoms are hitting you hard and the app will tweet out at your choice of representatives and congresspeople to remind them that reproductive rights cover a whole lot more issues than abortions.
